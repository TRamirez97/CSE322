Spaghetti and Meatballs

1. Place pot of water over a flame until water boils. Add salt for flavor.

2. When water boils, add uncooked spaghetti noodles to water and cook for 12 min or 11 min for al dente.

3. While cooking pasta, put sauce on mediuim heat.

4. Grab small amounts of ground beef, roll into a ball and proceed to add it to sauce to cook. You can also cook it on a pan over heat.

Country Fried Steak

1. Tenderize flat steak until it has a nice rigged texture. Aternatively buy some already tendereized cube steak.

2. Add olive oil or butter to warm pan and heat.

3. Dip steak into flour, milk and flour again to give it a coating.

4. Place steak on pan and cook over medium heat.

5. Cook until brown, remove from heatt and boom country fried steak.
