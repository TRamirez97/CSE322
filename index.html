Spaghetti and Meatballs

1. Place pot of water over a flame until water boils. Add salt for flavor.Help

2. When water boils, add uncooked spaghetti noodles to water and cook for 12 min or 11 min for al dente.

3. While cooking pasta, put sauce on mediuim heat.

4. Grab small amounts of ground beef, roll into a ball and proceed to add it to sauce to cook. You can also cook it on a pan over heat.
